Privacy Policy

About this policy

This document is the Privacy Policy for the Close Other Tabs Chrome Extension.

What information do we collect?

The Close Other Tabs Chrome Extension does not collect any information about you or your browsing behavior. The extension neither stores information locally nor sends any data to remote servers.

Cookies policy

The Close Other Tabs Chrome Extension does not use any cookies for tracking or any other purpose.

Changes to this policy

This policy may change at any time with or without a separate notice. You are encouraged to periodically review this Policy.
